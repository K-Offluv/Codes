About Us

Welcome to K-Offluv Investment Limited, a distinguished Ghanaian company which is the umbrella body of many subsidiary brands within our company. Some of our subsidiary brands are (K-Offluv Construction and Civil Engineer Services, K-Offluv Real Estate, K-Offluv Travel Consult, K-Offluv General Trading and K-Offluv Transport and Logistics).
K-Offluv Investment Limited was established in 2018 which provides construction services, Real Estate (buying and selling of properties), Selling of cars and car tyers. Now K-OFFLUV INVESTMENT LIMITED is currently focused on providing a broad mix of products and services tb its client /target market. Such as (Recruitment, Selling of Engine Oil and Transmission Oil, Exportation of Food and Cargo handling).

K-OFFLUV TRANSPORT AND LOGISTICS/HAULAGE

K-Offluv Investment Limited provides bussing services, intercity services, and transportation of cargos. We have acquired state-of-the-art brand-new crossover buses that can withstand all weather (14 seater, 32 seater and 50seater, fully air-conditioned and very spacious) and cargo vehicles for services.
K-Offluv Investment Limited has also made sure that its buses are comprehensively insured and has employed well-trained, educated, and experienced drivers for the venture. This puts us in a very flexible position — not only to respond to client (passenger) needs but also to project ourselves as a credible and safety-oriented transport service company.


Transportation of Goods:
• Move cargo via road, air or sea.
• Ensure vehicles and routes are suitable for the type of cargo (e.g., refrigerated, hazardous, oversized).
Route Planning & Optimization:
• Select efficient, cost-effective, and timely routes.
• Consider traffic, weather, fuel costs, and delivery deadlines.
• Optimize for backhauls to reduce empty return trips.
Loading & Unloading:
• Proper loading of goods to prevent damage and ensure vehicle stability.
• We Use the appropriate equipment for loading and Unloading (e.g. forklifts, cranes).
• K-Offluv Follows safety protocols and weight limits.
Warehousing & Storage:
• Store goods securely before transit or at distribution centers.
• Manage inventory, climate control, and cargo security.
• Tracking of goods using inventory management systems.
Packaging & Labeling:
• K-Offluv ensures that goods are packaged securely and appropriately before transporting.
• We attach correct labels: destination, handling instructions, barcodes making sure the goods reach it rightful destination/owners.
Documentation & Compliance:
• Generate and manage shipping documents: bills of lading, customs paperwork, invoices, etc.
• Ensure compliance with local, national, and international regulations.
• Handle customs clearance for international shipments.
Customer Service & Communication:
• We always keep clients informed about shipment status.
• Handle queries, complaints, and special requirements.
• Offer delivery confirmations and feedback options.
Safety Measures:
We have modern safety and tracking equipment at our disposal, mainly used for monitoring vehicles and drivers to ensure timely intervention when needed. The state of these devices is regularly maintained to ensure they function effectively to meet our clients’ needs.
Drivers:
K-Offluv Investment Limited drivers are sharp, energetic, innovative, and team-spirited. They are passion-driven, ensuring timeliness, safety, and high-quality service at all times.
